Flauta Solo

Flauta solo is an application for Statistical Analysis of Solo Flute pieces composed between 1710 and 1810 and available in the International Music Score Library Project (IMSLP).

This application is part of the research conducted by Genos Research Group and is being developed by Marcos da Silva Sampaio and Pedro Kroger.
